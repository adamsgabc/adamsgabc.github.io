About

Gabriela Adams

Mexican-American woman, born and raised in the South {Georgia, USA}, attended American School of Doha in the Middle-East {Doha, Qatar}.

This upcomming May 2022, I will obtain a Bachelor of Arts in Cognitive Science with the addition of a Minor in Computer Science {University of Georgia}.

Cognitive Science is an interdisciplinary field with its four pillars based in Artificial Intelligence, Philosophy, Psychology, and Language / Culture. UGA is one of the select few universities that offer Cognitive Science and have an exceptional program for Artificial Intelligence. It is the only university in Georgia that one can receive a degree in this field. To graduate with the program, students choose two of the four pillars for their concentration. I specialize in both Artificial Intelligence and Language / Culture.
